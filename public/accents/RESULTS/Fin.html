majority/plurality percentage for each speaker in boldface;

Points to note:

  • There is a general similarity to the New Zealand results, with the EE male high in the status variables, but low in solidarity.
  • The female EE voice is generally rated low throughout, almost as low as the NZE male voice.
  • As expected, both NAm voices are rated highly overall; the NAm female is the leader in almost all traits except the four associated with status.
