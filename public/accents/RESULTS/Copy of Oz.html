Points to note:

  • The AusE male is rated highly in status, above the RP-speaking EE male.
  • The AusE male and AusE female are rated highly in solidarity, but still well below the two NAm voices.
  • The NZE female is rated more highly in solidarity by the Australians than she is by the New Zealanders.
  • The EE female voice is rated lower in power than she is in New Zealand.
